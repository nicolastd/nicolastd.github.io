Main focus

My research focuses on the modeling and theory of the interaction between solids and intense laser fields. I am working on developing novel theoretical tools, such time-dependent density functional theory for strongly correlated systems and computational methods for the ab initio description of condensed matter coupled to intense light-field. My work aims at improving our understanding and control of the electron and spin dynamics on ultrafast timescales.
